Support Spacing — Cheat Sheet
NM, MC, EMT, IMC, RMC, FMC, PVC
▶
🟡 NM-B (Romex)
Max spacing54" (4.5 ft)
Near box/fitting12"
Staple flat to framing. Not in exposed/damp/wet locations. Holes through studs count as support.
⚪ MC Cable
Max spacing72" (6 ft)
Near box/fitting12"
Approved staples, straps, or hangers. Listed connectors required at each termination.
⚪ AC Cable (BX)
Max spacing54" (4.5 ft)
Near box/fitting12"
Anti-short bushing required at each termination end. Same support rules as NM-B.
🔵 EMT
Max spacing120" (10 ft)
Near box/fitting36"
All trade sizes: 10 ft max. One-hole straps, two-hole straps, or conduit hangers.
🔵 IMC
Max spacing120" (10 ft)
Near box/fitting36"
Same support rules as EMT. Coupling at 10 ft joints counts as a support point.
🔵 RMC
Max spacing120" (10 ft)
Near box/fitting36"
Rigid conduit at 10 ft sticks. Threaded coupling at each stick effectively becomes support.
🟠 FMC (Flex)
Max spacing54" (4.5 ft)
Near box/fitting12"
Max 6 ft flex whips for luminaires if securely fastened at fixture end. Listed fittings each end.
🟠 LFMC / LFNC
Max spacing54" (4.5 ft)
Near box/fitting12"
Liquidtight: up to 6 ft allowed at equipment terminals for vibration isolation. 3/8" max 6 ft.
